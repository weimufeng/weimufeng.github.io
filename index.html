About

I am Mufeng Wei, a battery research scientist at LiNova Energy Inc. Currently I work and live in Arcadia, California.

Before joing LiNova, I received my B.S. in Chemistry (first major) and Business Administration (second major) from Tsinghua University, and my Ph.D. in Chemistry from University of California, Berkeley.

My research and work interests broadly reside in the AI for science.

I enjoy playing basketball, video games, and k-pop. Favorite k-pop artists: IU, Jennie, Nayeon, and Eunchae.
